About our shared Journey

It was the beginning of March 2022 when the universe brought us together at Thrive, a small café in Southampton. We had been chatting online and decided to meet up, although neither of us were looking for a relationship at the time.

Despite divergent upbringings and pasts, we immediately connected over an uncannily similar inner journey. When we parted with a simple hug, it sparked something deep within us - a transformative force that left us completely stunned. In an instant, our lives had changed, and we had stepped through a portal to another world entirely.

From that unexpected moment on, things started to shift rapidly. Together, we explored exhilarating self-discovery, rewriting our once separate lives to forge a bold new shared path.

The Avatara Retreat

Tulum, Mexico

Nestled in the lush jungle of Tulum, Mexico lies a visionary space dedicated to cultivating harmony between humanity, nature and the universe.

Welcome to our project, The Avatara Retreat.

At the heart of this project we plan to build a beautifully crafted wooden pyramid, built with reverence for the land that sustains it. We fully embrace an ethos of living in resonance with the Earth. The pyramid structure keeps us connected to the rhythms of the natural world whilst allowing a gateway connecting us to the stars.

Within this graceful structure, we hold a vision of people coming together to uplift one another through transformative experiences that nurture the body, mind and spirit. We will be hosting events and retreats focused on holistic well-being, bringing people together and providing a welcoming space to allow others to host a range of conscious events.

Avatara Temple will also facilitate advanced Vibrational Self Expression Retreats, inviting participants to a 10-day immersive experience diving into the creation of music aligned with fifth dimensional frequencies. We envision people feeling profoundly rejuvenated by their return to nature, inspired by the beauty that surrounds them.

At its core, this is a heart-centered project. We nurture personal growth through compassion, joy and unity. Our highest intention is to be a space where people can experience their intrinsic wholeness while contributing to the well-being of all. We believe that when human hearts align, we gain the power to collectively transition toward a more conscious society in harmony with this beautiful planet that we call home.
